About Robots

Robots are intelligent machines designed to perform tasks automatically. They play a big role in industries, medicine, space exploration, and even entertainment.

Types of Robots

  • Industrial Robots: Used in factories for manufacturing tasks.
  • Medical Robots: Assist in surgery, rehabilitation, and diagnosis.
  • Service Robots: Help at home, hotels, and offices (like cleaning robots).
  • Military Robots: Used in defense, drones, and bomb disposal.
  • Space Robots: Like the Mars Rovers, exploring outer planets.

How Robots Work

Robots sense their environment using sensors, make decisions using software or AI, and move using motors and actuators. They are programmed to perform tasks efficiently and sometimes autonomously.
